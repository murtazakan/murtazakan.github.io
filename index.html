About Me

I had always been inspired by creativity be it in IT or workouts. It was during 2018, that i started to develop a passion for gaming. I wanted to have my own fighting game in market some day and being a noob I started my journey into gaming industries as a game tester at Ubisoft. After spending a year I learnt about how games be tested for quality and production lifecycle. I heard about Outscal after some time and finally I think I found my place where I needed to be in this gaming industry.Also my first fighting game prototype is WIP which is inspired by my love for anime.

I like to keep on refreshing my knowledge and skills by reading articles online and stay up to date with the gaming industry. I love to get feedback be it critical or compliment because I love getting to know my audience/clients.
